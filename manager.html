Project Manager Intern Application

<h2>

Job Description :


We are seeking an experienced Project Manager to oversee and manage the development and delivery of web development projects. The successful candidate will work closely with our Frontend and Backend Web Developers, as well as our Graphic Designer, to ensure that projects are delivered on-time, within budget, and meet the needs of our clients.



Key Responsibilities:


- Plan, execute, and manage multiple web development projects simultaneously.
- Work closely with clients to understand project requirements and manage expectations throughout the project lifecycle.
- Develop project plans, timelines, budgets, and resource allocation.
- Collaborate with Frontend and Backend Web Developers and Graphic Designer to ensure all project deliverables are met on-time and to a high level of quality.
- Manage project risks, issues, and changes to ensure project success.
- Provide regular project status updates to stakeholders and clients.
- Identify and implement process improvements to optimize project delivery.



Education Qualification :


Should be pursuing Bachelor's degree in any field.



Primary Skills Requirement :


- Proven experience in project management with a focus on web development projects.
- Strong understanding of web development methodologies and technologies.
- Familiarity with project management tools such as Asana, Trello, or Jira.
- Excellent communication and leadership skills.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.



Secondary Skills Requirement : (not mandatory):


- - Familiarity with UX design principles and best practices.
- Experience in managing remote teams.
