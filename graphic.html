Graphic Designer Intern Application

<h2>

Job Description :


We are seeking a talented Graphic Designer to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for creating visually appealing and effective graphics, illustrations, and other design elements that communicate our brand and message to our audience.



Key Responsibilities:


- Design and create graphics, illustrations, logos, and other design elements for a variety of mediums including print and digital.
- Collaborate with the marketing team to develop design concepts that effectively communicate our brand and message to our audience.
- Create visual designs that adhere to brand guidelines and standards.
- Produce and manage final artwork for print and digital media.
- Stay up-to-date with emerging trends in graphic design and apply them to improve design elements.



Education Qualification :


Should be pursuing Bachelor's degree in any field.



Primary Skills Requirement :


- Proven experience in graphic design with a strong portfolio showcasing various design projects.
- Proficient in design software such as Adobe Photoshop, or Figma.
- Familiarity with design principles such as color theory, typography, and composition.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent communication and teamwork skills.



Secondary Skills Requirement : (not mandatory):


- Experience in designing for a variety of mediums including print and digital.
- Experience with motion graphics and video editing.
- Familiarity with UX design principles and best practices.
- Ability to work independently and manage multiple projects simultaneously
